Nursing home covid

The US Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services puts out a dataset of covid incidence, deaths, and vaccination at 13751 US nursing homes. The data are available at https://data.cms.gov/covid-19/covid-19-nursing-home-data. It has info on individual nursing homes, but it can be a little hard to use. I am making it more usable here, trying to make it easier for folks to see how people in nursing homes are being cared for. This uses federal data from the week ending 12/10/23.

The tabs above have different views of the data. If you want to look at detail on a particular facility, it is probably easiest to use the map or table pages. Some quick summaries:

  • 6029 nursing homes have zero healthcare staff fully up to date on covid vaccines.

  • 72 nursing homes have all healthcare staff fully up to date on covid vaccines.

  • 7% of nursing home healthcare staff nationwide are fully up to date on covid vaccines.

  • 83% of of nursing home healthcare staff nationwide ever got vaccinated for covid.

  • 3655 nursing homes have zero residents fully up to date on covid vaccines.

  • 95 nursing homes have all residents fully up to date on covid vaccines.

  • Only 32.2% of nursing home residents are fully up to date on covid vaccines.
